About me

I'm a Master's student in Computer Science at Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the guidance of Dr. Bohua Zhan. I am in the State Key Lab. of Computer Science, researching on formal methods, leaded by Prof. Naijun Zhan. My research focuses on theorem proving on hybrid systems. And I'm going to focus on safe reinforcement learning in the new semester.
